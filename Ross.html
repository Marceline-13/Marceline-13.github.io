Alex Ross

Born: Jan. 22, 1970, Portland, Oregon


Areas Worked In: Painter and Illustrator


Notable Works: "Kingdom Come", "Worlds Greatest Super-Heroes", "Marvels"

Kingdom Come


Alex Ross is well known for creating realistic interpretations of iconic Super-Heroes. Ross brings these famous characters to life with mixes of different paints such as gouache and wash that he uses to hand paint each individual comic panel. He is often know to use pictures of himself dressed and posed as iconic heroes as his refence.
