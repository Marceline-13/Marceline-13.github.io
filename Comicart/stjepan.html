Stjepan Šejić

Born: Nov. 7, 1981, Vinkovci, Yugoslavia


Areas Worked In: Illustrator and Writer.


Notable Works: "Sunstone", "Harleen", "Fine Print"

Sunstone Vol. 1 Cover


Stjepan uses digital art to create an almost oil painted look to his art. His characters are unique for their soft appearence and bright, emotion filled faces. Stjepan worked on the monumental comic series "Harleen" that dives deep into the popular character's Joker and Harley Quinn and their psychology. Currently Stjepan has chosen to step away from working with more corporate comic companies like DC in favor of working on his own indipend series such as "Sunstone."
