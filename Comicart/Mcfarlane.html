Todd Mcfarlane

Born: Mar. 16, 1961, Alberta, Canada


Areas Worked In: Penciller, Inker, Writer, and Publisher


Notable Works: "Spawn", "The Amazing Spiderman", "The Incredible Hulk", "Haunt"

Spawn #1 Cover


Todd Mcfarlane has a more "harsh" artsyle. He uses strong, sharp lines and heavy shading to make his characters look striking and imposing. While starting comic art, Todd found great inspiration from artists such as Frank Miller and Jack Kirby. Todd is responsible for creating the largely popular and still used modern appearence of Spiderman's nemesis "Venom" with sharp teeth and long snaking tongue. He is also credited with creating popular character and series "Spawn" and being partial founder of indie comic publisher "Image."
