Mike Mignola

Born: Sept. 16, 1960, Berkely, California


Areas Worked In: Writer, Penciller, and Inker


Notable works: "Hellboy", "B.P.R.D", "The Doom that Came to Gotham"

Hellboy: Seed of Destruction Cover Art


Mike Mignola's art style is often described as "dark" and "gothic". He uses heavy shading to his advantage to create a more impactful shadowy appearence. Often found within his comics is imagery heavely inspired by the cosmic creatures described in the works of H.P. Lovecraft.
